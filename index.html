Staff AI Engineer, Sony AI
Ph.D., Boston University |  Former Postdoc, MIT CSAIL

My research is at the intersection of formal methods, control theory, and robotics. I develop provably correct and safe controllers for complex dynamical systems, drawing on optimization, hybrid systems, and temporal logic.
