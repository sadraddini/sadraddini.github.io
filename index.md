# Sadra Sadraddini – Roboticist

Welcome to my website! Here is some information about me and my work.

I am currently a Staff AI Engineer at [Sony AI](https://ai.sony).

Previously, I was a principal robotics engineer at [Dexai Robotics](https://www.dexai.com). Before that, I held a postdoctoral associate position at Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence Lab ([CSAIL](https://www.csail.mit.edu)) at Massachusetts Institute of Technology ([MIT](http://mit.edu)). I was a member of [Robot Locomotion Group](http://groups.csail.mit.edu/locomotion/index.html), led by professor [Russ Tedrake](http://groups.csail.mit.edu/locomotion/russt.html). I got my Ph.D. in mechanical engineering in January 2018 at Boston University ([BU](http://bu.edu)). I was a member of [Hybrid Systems and Networked Systems Group](http://sites.bu.edu/hyness/) in [BU Robotics Lab](http://sites.bu.edu/robotics/), led by professor [Calin Belta](http://sites.bu.edu/hyness/calin/).

My work and research focus on dynamics and control of robots, with a focus on optimization-based algorithms, formal methods, and hybrid systems.
